Our Story

About Us

35 Years

Founded in 1985 by a small group of families seeking to create a welcoming faith community.

Faith Community Church has grown from humble beginnings in a living room to a thriving church family. For over three decades, we've been committed to serving our community, growing in faith together, and creating a place where everyone feels welcome.

Today, we continue this tradition while adapting to the needs of our growing and diverse community.
